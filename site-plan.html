Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Lil Gloomy is to make typing practice more fun and engaging by introducing gamified elements such as a leaderboard, currency, and a customizable experience. The site is designed to improve typing skills while keeping users motivated.

Audience

The target audience includes students, professionals, and anyone who wants to improve their typing skills in a fun and interactive way. The gamified elements make it especially appealing to younger audiences and gamers.

Dynamic elements

  • Leaderboard: A dynamic leaderboard tracks and displays the top players' typing scores.
  • Currency System: Users earn "Gloom Coins" through typing activities, which can be spent on skins and other customizable features.
  • Heat Map: A visual heat map shows areas where users make the most typing errors, helping them focus on improvement.
  • Interactive Store: A store dynamically updates based on available skins and items that can be purchased with Gloom Coins.

Content

Home page

The home page will introduce Lil Gloomy, explain its features, and provide access to other sections like the leaderboard, store, and heat map. It will include a short tutorial on how to start practicing typing and earning Gloom Coins.

Leaderboard

The leaderboard page will display a real-time ranking of users based on their typing scores, with a filter to show global rankings, friends' rankings, or personal bests.

Store

The store page will allow users to spend their Gloom Coins on character skins and customizable backgrounds for their typing interface.

Heat Map

The heat map page will visually display typing statistics, showing areas where users commonly make errors, helping them focus on those specific areas for improvement.
